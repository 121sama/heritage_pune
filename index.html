Pune Through the Ages

Journey through Pune's rich history from ancient times to the modern era

937 CE

Rashtrakuta Dynasty

Pune finds its first mention in historical records during the Rashtrakuta period, establishing its significance in the Deccan region.

1595

Mughal Influence

The region comes under Mughal control, leading to architectural and cultural developments that shaped medieval Pune.

1674

Shivaji's Capital

Chhatrapati Shivaji establishes Pune as a key center of the Maratha Empire, marking the beginning of its golden age.

1730

Peshwa Era Begins

The Peshwas make Pune their capital, leading to unprecedented growth in culture, architecture, and trade.

1818

British Colonial Period

British rule brings modern infrastructure, education systems, and administrative changes to Pune.

1947

Independent India

Post-independence Pune emerges as an educational and industrial hub, attracting students and businesses from across India.

1990s

IT Revolution

Pune transforms into India's IT capital, balancing modern technology with its rich cultural heritage.
